Dreamland

Where Dreams Come to Life

Problems

In Hyderabad, there are very few entertainment places such as farmhouses, adventure parks, horse riding, or go-karting. Families and individuals have limited options for fun.

Solution

We are constructing Dreamland a one-stop destination for round-the-clock entertainment, adventure, and luxury. It will include a golf club, water park, artificial sea, swimming pools, slides, indoor and outdoor sports (cricket, bowling, horse riding, ATV bikes, go-karting), a 5-star hotel, spa, and a dedicated area for kids. games and fun-filled activities.

About Resort

Dreamland is a family Resort in Hyderabad that provides attractions for all ages and offer guests with amenities of their choice including loads of family entertainment, artificial beach access, water recreation, dining, golf and lots more.

Target Market

  • Childrens
  • Teenagers
  • Adults and Families

Business Model

  1. Ticket sales for activities and events
  2. Room bookings
  3. Online membership and registration

Our Team

We have a large, skilled, and passionate team working to make Dreamland a reality including architects, engineers, designers, marketers, and customer service professionals.

Vision for the next 5 years

In the next five years, Dreamland will become Pakistan's biggest and most popular adventureland resort a go-to destination for fun, luxury, and lasting memories.
